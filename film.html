Howl's Moving Castle

The film is set in a fictional kingdom where both magic and early twentieth-century technology are prevalent, against the backdrop of a war with another kingdom. It tells the story of Sophie, a young milliner who is turned into an elderly woman by a witch who enters her shop and curses her.

Release date:2004

Director: Hayao Miyazaki

Cast:Chieko Baisho,Takuya Kimura,Akihiro Miwa
